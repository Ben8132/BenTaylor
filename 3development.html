Rise Insight day at KPMG

I went to the KPMG offices in Birmingham for the Rise Insight event which lasted 7 hours

The Rise Insight day showed me how large companies such as KPMG operate, their candiate selection and interview process, the skills and qualities that they value and what professional standards and the work that is expected. By better understanding how the selection process works from the companies perspective and the skills they look for has helped me to emprove my own employability and the skills that are neccesary.
